FASTER OPD REGISTRATION

The OPD registration has been made simple, seamless and accurate by the Scan and Share functionality.

The Scan and Share service allows patients to scan the unique QR code of the hospital using any ABDM-enabled application (such as Aarogya Setu, ABHA app, EkaCare, DRiefcase, Bajaj Health, PayTM and others) and share their ABHA profile with the hospital.

After sharing the details, the patient can simply visit the registration counter, collect the outpatient slip and visit the doctor without waiting in a long queue.

How it works ?

Step 1

Scan The QR Code

Scan the QR code using any QR code scanner, phone camera, or any ABDM-enabled app available on your smartphone.

Step 2

Install ABDM Enabled App

Install any ABDM-enabled Personal Health Records app (if not already installed), then register or log in with your mobile number.

Step 3

Share Profile

Share your ABHA profile with the hospital and generate the registration token instantly.

Step 4

Get OP Slip

Visit the registration counter, show the token, collect your OP slip and proceed to visit the doctor.

Features of Online Registration System

Online Registration System (ORS) is a framework to link various hospitals across the country for online appointment systems where counter-based OPD registration and appointment systems through Hospital Management Information Systems (HMIS) have been digitalized. The application has been hosted on the cloud services of NIC. The portal facilitates online appointments with various departments of different Hospitals using ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account).

Simple Appointment Process

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What is the Online Registration System (ORS)?

ORS is a digital platform that allows patients to book OPD appointments, view lab reports, and access healthcare services online without visiting the hospital physically.

Q2. Do I need an ABHA (Health ID) to register?

No, you can register with your mobile number. However, having an ABHA ID helps you access integrated digital health records.

Q3. How can I book an online appointment?

Click on Register/Login, enter your mobile number, verify the OTP, select the hospital, choose a department, and book your preferred slot.

Q4. Is there any registration fee?

No, online registration and appointment booking are completely free.

Q5. I did not receive my OTP. What should I do?

Wait for a few minutes and try again. You can also check network coverage or switch to another mobile number.

Q6. Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Yes. Visit the Dashboard after logging in and choose the option to cancel or reschedule your appointment.
