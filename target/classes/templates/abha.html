Create Ayushman Bharat Health Account - ABHA (Health ID)

ABHA number is a 14 digit number that will uniquely identify you as a participant in India's digital healthcare ecosystem. It will help you establish a strong and trustable identity that will be accepted by healthcare providers and payers across the country.

Benefits of ABHA Health ID card

  • ✔
    Unique & Trustable Identity
    Establish unique identity across different healthcare providers within the healthcare ecosystem.
  • ✔
    Unified Benefits
    Link public health programmes and insurance schemes to your unique ABHA number.
  • ✔
    Hassle-free Access
    Avoid long queues for registration by using your ABHA Health ID for OPD visits.
