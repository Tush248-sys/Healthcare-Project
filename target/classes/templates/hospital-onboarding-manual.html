Hospital Onboarding Manual

This guide explains how hospitals can register on the Online Healthcare Registration Portal and start offering online OPD appointments, manage queues, and integrate with digital health services.

1. Requirements Before Onboarding

  • Hospital registration certificate
  • Complete address and location details
  • Official hospital email ID and phone number
  • Nodal Officer details
  • List of departments and OPD timings
  • Digital infrastructure (computers, internet, printers, registration counters)
  • ABHA / Health ID integration readiness (if applicable)

2. Steps to Start Hospital Onboarding

  1. Visit the portal home page.
  2. Scroll to the Hospital Onboarding section.
  3. Click ORS OnBoarding Manual to read this guide.
  4. Click Hospital Join Free to open the registration form.
  5. Already registered hospitals should click Hospital Login.

3. Information Collected in Hospital Join Free

Hospital Details

Hospital name, type (Government / Private / Trust / Autonomous), category, registration or licence number, year of establishment.

Address & Location

Complete postal address, state, district, PIN code, and optional map coordinates.

Contact Information

Primary and secondary phone numbers, official email address, emergency contact details if applicable.

Nodal Officer

Full name, designation, mobile number, email ID, and supporting identity proof.

Department & OPD Setup

Department names, specialist availability, OPD timings, weekly off days, and maximum patients per shift.

Digital Infrastructure & Uploads

Availability of computers, internet, printers/scanners, ABHA readiness, IT staff, and document uploads such as registration certificate and authorization letters.

4. Verification & Approval

After submission, the portal team reviews the data. In some cases, details may be verified with the State Health Authority. The hospital will receive confirmation or a request for clarification via email/SMS.

Once approved, credentials are shared to allow login to the Hospital Dashboard where departments, timings and appointments can be managed.
